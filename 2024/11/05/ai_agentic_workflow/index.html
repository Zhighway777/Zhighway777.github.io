AI_Agentic_Workflow

By Zeng

November 5, 2024

Why do this?

Now, the apperance of LLM model such as GPT-4, llama3 has brought breakthroughs in the field of AI. However, the tradional LLM interaction is more like a “Non-agent workflow”, where users input the instructions and wait for the output after some time. This lack of room for iterative optimization.

AI Agent breaks the deadlock by treating the AI-system as one could think, plan and perform tasks, just like an autonomous decision-making Agent.

Related link: Andrew Ng’s presentation ar Developer Day at the 2024 Snowflake Summit

How to design?

To achieve that goal, the key is Agentic workflow. The core insight of Agentic workflow is to break down complex tasks into multiple simple steps. The results are optimized step by step in by loops. Common steps includes:

  1. Setting Goal

  2. Plan and Decompose

  3. Iterative Execution

Agentic vs Agent

“Agent” is a noun, implying a binary division of black and white, such as “this system is not an Agent” and “LLM is an Agent”. While “Agentic” is an objective, representing a concept of degree.

An Agent receives input and performs the data by preset rules, then giving the result. But Agentic AI is not limited by passive instructions, instead they can actively perceive the environment, understand the goal, and independently choose a series action.
